What happens in the background

The app detects hand landmarks, classifies the sign with the trained model, and instantly converts it into English text. Voice input is transcribed into text for the Deaf or Mute user. Training data comes from online sign language datasets that support the learning modules.

  • Video capture identifies hand movements in real time.
  • Gesture model maps signs to English text.
  • Speech output reads translated text aloud for listeners.
